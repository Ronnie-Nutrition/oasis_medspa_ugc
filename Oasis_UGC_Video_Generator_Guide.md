## What Is the UGC Video Generator?

The UGC Video Generator is your personal AI-powered video creation tool. It automatically creates short-form social media videos featuring realistic AI presenters promoting Oasis Luxury Med Spa services.

**What it does for you:**

- Creates professional "talking head" videos in under 2 minutes
- Features 30 diverse AI presenters (different ages, ethnicities, and roles)
- Writes the script automatically based on your topic
- Generates a realistic presenter image
- Adds natural-sounding voiceover
- Animates the presenter with lip-synced speech
- Saves the finished video to your Google Drive
- Logs every video in your Google Sheet for easy tracking

**Perfect for:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Stories, and YouTube Shorts.

## How to Create a Video

### Step 1: Open the Form

Open your video generator form link in any web browser (works on phone or computer):

> **Your form URL will be provided by your administrator**

### Step 2: Fill Out the Form

The form has 6 fields:

| Field | What to Choose |
|-------|---------------|
| **Avatar Selection** | Pick a specific presenter by name, or choose **"Auto-Rotate (Surprise Me)"** to let the system cycle through all 30 |
| **Video Type** | Choose the style of video (see options below) |
| **Topic/Service** | Select which med spa service to promote |
| **Video Length** | Choose **5 seconds**, **10 seconds**, or **15 seconds** |
| **Custom Message** | *(Optional)* Type your own script. Leave blank to let AI write it |
| **Background Style** | Pick the setting behind the presenter |

### Video Types Explained

- **Call to Action** — Promotes booking/contacting Oasis. Includes phone number and website. Best for driving appointments.
- **Tips & Education** — Shares skincare/wellness advice. Positions Oasis as the expert. Great for engagement.
- **Before & After** — Highlights transformation results. Builds excitement. Best for showcasing treatments.
- **Service Spotlight** — Introduces a specific service and its benefits. Perfect for educating new clients.

### Background Styles

- **Luxury Spa Interior** — Warm lighting, marble accents, greenery
- **Clean Clinical** — Modern white, professional medical aesthetic
- **Elegant Treatment Room** — Plush chair, calming colors, skincare products
- **Neutral Studio** — Clean golden glow, simple and elegant
- **Oasis Branded (Gold Logo Center)** — Features the Oasis gold logo on the wall
- **Oasis Branded (Logo Watermark Corner)** — Subtle Oasis logo in the corner

### Step 3: Submit and Wait

After you click **Submit**:

1. The AI writes a script (or uses your custom message)
2. A realistic presenter image is generated
3. Professional voiceover is created
4. The presenter is animated with lip-synced speech
5. The video is uploaded to your Google Drive
6. A log entry is added to your Google Sheet

**This takes about 1-2 minutes.** You'll find your video in Google Drive when it's done.

## How to Download Videos to Your Phone

### Option A: From the Google Sheet (Easiest)

1. Open the **Google Sheets app** on your phone (free from App Store / Google Play)
2. Open your UGC Video Generator spreadsheet
3. Find the video you want in the list
4. Tap the **Drive URL** link in that row
5. The video will open in Google Drive
6. Tap the **three dots menu** (top right)
7. Tap **"Download"** or **"Save to device"**
8. The video saves to your phone's camera roll / gallery

### Option B: From Google Drive Directly

1. Open the **Google Drive app** on your phone
2. Navigate to your Oasis UGC video folder
3. Find the video you want
4. Tap the **three dots** next to the file name
5. Tap **"Download"** or **"Make available offline"**
6. The video saves to your phone

### Option C: Share Directly to Social Media

1. Open the video in Google Drive on your phone
2. Tap the **three dots menu**
3. Tap **"Send a copy"** or **"Share"**
4. Choose Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or any other app
5. Post directly — no download needed!

## Tips for Best Results

**For the best-looking videos:**

- **10-second videos** tend to look the most natural and perform best on social media
- **Use "Auto-Rotate"** to get variety in your content — different faces keep your feed fresh
- **Oasis Branded backgrounds** are great for building brand recognition
- **Leave "Custom Message" blank** most of the time — the AI writes great scripts. Use custom only when you need a very specific message

**For social media posting:**

- Post **3-5 videos per week** for best engagement
- Mix up your video types — alternate between CTAs, Tips, and Spotlights
- **Tips & Education** videos tend to get the most organic reach
- **Call to Action** videos are best for driving bookings
- Add your own captions and hashtags when posting
